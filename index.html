01 | Overview

What is the Creator Program?

The Sogni Creator Program is designed for dedicated creators who actively use Sogni, produce high‑quality work, and want to contribute to the growth of the platform.

🏆

Support & Reward

Support and reward top creators who consistently produce strong work with Sogni across all creative categories.

💬

Product Feedback

Collect actionable product feedback from experienced users to help improve the platform and drive its evolution.

🤝

Collaborative Growth

A collaborative program built around creativity, quality, and product improvement, empowering creators and the platform together.

04 | Expectations

What Sogni Expects

Creators accepted into the program are expected to contribute in two key areas: high‑quality creative output and monthly product feedback.

🎨

A. High-Quality Creative Output

Creators are expected to produce work that demonstrates the capabilities of Sogni. There is no required content category or style. All strong creative work is welcome.

Accepted formats include:

👻 Horror ⛩️ Anime 🌟 Pop Culture 😏 Satire 🏔️ Landscapes 🧪 Experimental ✨ Other Original

The evaluation standard is quality and originality, not genre.

📝

B. Monthly Product Feedback

Creators are expected to provide regular, constructive feedback on the Sogni product experience. This feedback is reviewed directly by the team and influences product decisions.

  • What is working well
  • What is frustrating or unclear
  • What should be improved
  • What features or tools they would like to see built
05 | Positioning

Program Positioning

Understanding what this program is, and what it isn't, is essential to maintaining its integrity and the authenticity of our creator community.

✅ What This Program Is

  • A creator support and recognition program
  • A feedback channel between experienced users and the Sogni team
  • A way to highlight high‑quality creative work made with Sogni
  • A framework for encouraging authentic visibility through strong content

🚫 What This Program Is Not

  • A paid shilling or forced promotion program
  • Spamming communities with low-quality content
  • Posting inauthentic promotional material
  • Misrepresenting opinions about the product

Creators are welcome to mention Sogni naturally when sharing their work, but authenticity is essential. The goal is to showcase strong work first, with product mention occurring organically where relevant.

06 | KPIs

Monthly Requirements

To remain in good standing, creators are expected to meet the following monthly KPIs and maintain consistent quality across all submitted content.

KPI Requirement Details
External Posts Min. 10 / month Qualifying posts featuring work created with Sogni
URL Submissions Min. 10 / month Submit URLs for monthly review by end of each month
Quality Standard Consistent Maintain consistent quality across all submitted content
Distribution External Share content in relevant external communities & platforms
Compliance Verified All submissions must be authentic, verifiable, and compliant

📋 Qualifying Post Criteria

  • Published in a relevant external community or platform
  • Features meaningful creative output made with Sogni
  • Meets the expected quality standard
  • Represents authentic participation, not spam
  • Suitable for manual review by moderators
07 | Process

Monthly Submission & Review

The monthly cycle is straightforward: create, submit, review, and earn. Here's how it works step by step.

01

Create & Publish

Creators publish qualifying content throughout the month across relevant external communities and platforms.

02

Submit Links

By the end of each month, creators submit their post links through the designated form for review.

03

Moderator Review

Moderators review submissions for quality, authenticity, relevance, and compliance with program standards.

04

Rewards Distribution

Rewards are distributed within the first two weeks of the following month based on the evaluation.

08 | Reward Calculation

How Rewards Are Determined

Monthly rewards are not based solely on post count. Reward allocation is determined proportionally, rewarding both quality and consistency.

Factor Impact
Number of qualifying submissions More qualifying posts contribute to higher reward allocation
Quality of submissions Higher-quality work leads to stronger reward outcomes
Overall value & impact The overall contribution and impact of the creator's monthly work

Lower activity or lower quality may result in a reduced reward. Higher-quality and more consistent contributions may lead to stronger reward outcomes.

09 | Program Rules

General Program Rules

All participants are expected to maintain professional standards and act with integrity throughout the program.

Participant Responsibilities

  • Follow community and platform rules
  • Maintain respectful and professional conduct
  • Submit only genuine and verifiable work
  • Avoid spammy, misleading, or low-effort promotion
  • Represent their use of Sogni honestly
  • Comply with moderator review and program decisions

Sogni Reserves the Right To

  • Review all submissions manually
  • Reject submissions below quality/authenticity standards
  • Adjust eligibility requirements
  • Update KPI expectations
  • Modify reward structures
  • Remove participants who don't meet program standards

A list of recommended AI communities and relevant external channels will be shared in the #Sogni Creator channel on Discord.

10 | Performance Standards

Performance & Removal Policy

Creators are expected to maintain the program's monthly KPI and quality standards on an ongoing basis.

⚠️ Two-Month Policy

If a creator fails to meet the required KPI or program standards for two consecutive months, Sogni reserves the right to remove that creator from the program. This approach is intended to provide flexibility while maintaining a consistent standard. Removed creators may be considered again in the future at Sogni's discretion.
